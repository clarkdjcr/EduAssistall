Safety & Compliance

Five stages between a student and any AI response

Every student message passes through a mandatory sequential safety pipeline before the AI is called — and the AI's reply is checked again before it reaches the student.

Stage 1

Input Classification

Violence, weapons, drugs, and sexual content are blocked before the AI ever sees the message.

Stage 2

PII Redaction

Names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, and other identifiers are stripped and never sent to the AI model.

Stage 3

Distress Detection

Emotional distress signals skip the AI entirely. A counsellor alert fires and the student receives a warm, human-crafted response.

Stage 4

Output Classification

The AI's reply is screened for harmful content before delivery. Flagged responses are replaced with a safe fallback.

Stage 5

Frustration Detection

Repeated rephrasing and negative sentiment fire educator alerts in the background — without ever slowing down the student.
