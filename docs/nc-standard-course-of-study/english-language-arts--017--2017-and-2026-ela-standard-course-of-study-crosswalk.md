# English Language Arts - 2017 and 2026 ELA Standard Course of Study Crosswalk

Grades 11-12

NORTH CAROLINA STANDARD COURSE OF STUDY
Crosswalk
English Language Arts Grades 11-12

The purpose of this document is to provide a general comparison of the 2017 English Language Arts Standard Course of Study and the 2026 English Language Arts Standard Course of Study.  It provides initial insight into similarities and differences between these two sets of standards. As part of the revision process, standards from the 2017 ELA SCOS that contained multiple or repeated skills were integrated across the new strands in the 2026 ELA SCOS; therefore, some 2017 standards are recorded multiple times in the crosswalk.

Note: The 2026 ELA standards and objectives are not intended to be the curriculum, nor do they indicate the whole of a curriculum which will be written by a local public-school unit (PSU) or school. The standards for this course have been developed to serve as the framework which will guide each PSU in the development of the curriculum for grades 11-12 English Language Arts.

NORTH CAROLINA STANDARD COURSE OF STUDY
Crosswalk
English Language Arts, Kindergarten

The purpose of this document is to provide a general comparison of the 2017 English Language Arts Standard Course of Study and the 2026 English Language Arts Standard Course of Study.  It provides initial insight into similarities and differences between these two sets of standards. As part of the revision process, standards from the 2017 ELA SCOS that contained multiple or repeated skills were integrated across the new strands in the 2026 ELA SCOS; therefore, some 2017 standards are recorded multiple times in the crosswalk.

Note: The 2026 ELA standards and objectives are not intended to be the curriculum, nor do they indicate the whole of a curriculum which will be written by a local public-school unit (PSU) or school. The standards for this course have been developed to serve as the framework which will guide each PSU in the development of the curriculum for Kindergarten English Language Arts.
