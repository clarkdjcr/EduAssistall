## About the NCDPI World Languages Team
The NCDPI World Languages Team provides Public School Units (PSUs) with support related to World Language standards, curriculum, and instruction. The work of the NCDPI World Languages Team includes: sustaining statewide implementation of the [2024 Standard Course of Study for World Languages](https://go.ncdpi.gov/2024NCSCOSWL), developing resources, providing professional development and technical assistance, and coordinating efforts to build stakeholder capacity.
Contact the NCDPI World Languages Team for more information or to request support for your PSU.
