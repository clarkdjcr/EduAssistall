## About The NCDPI ELA Team
The NCDPI ELA Team provides Public School Units (PSUs) with support related to ELA standards, curriculum, and instruction. The work the NCDPI ELA Team includes: sustaining statewide implementation of the NC ELA Standards, developing resources, providing professional development and technical assistance, and coordinating efforts to build stakeholder capacity. Contact the NCDPI ELA Team for more information or to request support for your PSU.
