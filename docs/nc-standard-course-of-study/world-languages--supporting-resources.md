## World Languages Standards & Instructional Toolkit Resources

### Teacher & Classroom Resources
The [Teacher & Classroom Resources section](https://ncdpi.instructure.com/courses/6912/pages/teacher-and-classroom-resources)of the World Languages Resources Hub has standards-based resources developed for K-12 World Language classrooms for use by individual teachers, departments, charter schools, districts, etc. and include:
- Navigator documents for standards-based local curriculum writing
- Language-specific information with online curriculum resources
- LinguaFolio as a formative assessment tool for language learning
### Administrator & Program Resources
The [Administrator & Program Resources section](https://ncdpi.instructure.com/courses/6912/pages/administrator-and-program-resources)of the World Languages Resources Hub has standards-based resources developed for K-12 World Language programs for use by department heads, building administrators, central office coordinators and administrators, and other stakeholders, and include:
- Administrator Guides for World Languages
- Advanced Programs information (AP, CIE, and IB)
- Crosswalks of World Language High School Courses with Community College Courses, the NCDPI Approved Assessments for the GLE, and World Language Course Codes for CDM
- Global Education materials about International Partnerships through North Carolina's MOUs
- Communications area where the archived announcements and newsletters from the World Language listservs are available
