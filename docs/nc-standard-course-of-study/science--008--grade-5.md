# Science - Grade 5

NORTH CAROLINA STANDARD COURSE OF STUDY

Grade 5 Science Crosswalk
The purpose of this document is to provide a general comparison of the 2009 Grade 5 Science Standard Course of Study and the 2023 Grade 5 Science Standard Course of Study.  It provides initial insight into similarities and differences between these two sets of standards. This document is not intended to answer all questions about the nuances of the new 2023 standards versus the previous 2009 standards.
Grade 5 Science Standards
Note: The 2023 Grade 5 standards and objectives are not intended to be the curriculum, nor do they constitute the entire curriculum to be written by a local public-school unit (PSU) or school. The standards for this course have been developed to serve as the framework which will guide each PSU in the development of the curriculum for Grade 5.
