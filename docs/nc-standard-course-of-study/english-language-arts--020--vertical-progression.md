# English Language Arts - Vertical Progression

Highlighted Vertical Progression

Introduction

This Highlighted Standards Progression Tool provides a vertical view of the NC Standard Course of Study for English Language Arts, K-12 (2026). These standards will be implemented in all North Carolina schools beginning in the 2027-28 school year.

This resource allows educators to see how each standard and objective changes and builds from grade-to-grade as they progress towards career and college readiness.  Educators can use the vertical progression to align and differentiate curriculum and instruction, develop scaffolds and supports, and coordinate curriculum and instruction across grades.

Statements are provided in a column to the left of the objectives in the Complex Texts (CT) Strand, Comprehension Development (CD) Strand, and Communication and Writing (CW) Strand in order to help educators understand the overarching focus for objectives as they progress across grades.  In order to support understanding of the vertical progression of skills in Comprehension Development Foundational Skills (CD.F), objectives are divided into required skills and their corresponding concepts. Similarly, CW.2 and CW.3 objectives are embedded within tables, mapping their initial introduction and continued use.
