K-12 General Music Crosswalk
The purpose of this document is to provide a general comparison of the 2010 General Music Standard Course of Study and the 2024 General Music Standard Course of Study. It provides initial insight into similarities and differences between these two sets of standards. This document is not intended to answer all questions about the nuances of the new standards versus the old.
