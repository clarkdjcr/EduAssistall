# Healthful Living - Health Education Glossary

North Carolina Standard Course of Study Health Education Glossary
Purpose
The Glossary of Terms seeks to address critical terms and definitions essential in building content knowledge and understanding but also in promoting consistency across health education, increased student outcomes, and improved parent communication about the implementation of the 2024 NC Standard Courses of Study in Healthful Living. These standards will be implemented in all North Carolina schools beginning in the 2025-26 school year.
