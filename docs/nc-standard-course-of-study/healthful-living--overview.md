### About the Healthful Living Team
The Healthful Living Team’s purpose is to provide support for North Carolina public school Health & Physical Education teachers. Contact the team for more information or to request support for your PSU.
### Resources
[Access the Healthful Living Hub](https://ncdpi.instructure.com/courses/12887)for a variety of resources supporting administrators, educators and parent/caregivers in implementation of the 2024 Healthful Living Standard Course of Study. This repository can be accessed with or without a Canvas account.
