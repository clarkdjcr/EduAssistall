## About the School Counseling Team
The School Counseling Team’s purpose is to provide support for North Carolina public school counselors and educator partners for best practices for comprehensive school counseling. This website serves as a central hub for school counselors, administrators, educators, institutions of higher education, and others interested in school counseling. On this site, you will find materials, resources, and professional development information related to the implementation of the North Carolina Standard Course of Study (SCoS) for NC Student Success Standards.
Contact the team for more information or to request support for your PSU.
