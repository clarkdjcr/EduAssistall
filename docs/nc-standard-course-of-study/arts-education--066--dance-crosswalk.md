# Arts Education - Dance Crosswalk

North Carolina Standard Course Of Study
K-12 Dance Crosswalk

The purpose of this document is to provide a general comparison of the 2010 Dance Standard Course of Study and the 2024 Dance Standard Course of Study.  It provides initial insight into similarities and differences between these two sets of standards. This document is not intended to answer all questions about the nuances of the new standards versus the old.

Kindergarten Dance Standards

Note: The 2024 Dance standards and objectives are not intended to be the curriculum, nor do they indicate the whole of a curriculum which will be written by a local public-school unit (PSU) or school. The standards for this course have been developed to serve as the framework which will guide each PSU in the development of the curriculum for Kindergarten Dance.
