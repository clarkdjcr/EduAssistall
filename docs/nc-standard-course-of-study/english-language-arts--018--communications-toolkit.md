# English Language Arts - Communications Toolkit

2026 ELA SCOS Communications Toolkit

Purpose
On January 8, 2026, the North Carolina State Board of Education approved Draft 4 as the 2026 English Language Arts (ELA) Standard Course of Study (SCOS). This toolkit is designed to help public school units (PSUs) communicate the newly adopted 2026 ELA SCOS to stakeholders. It provides ready-to-use templates, key messages, an FAQ, and visual materials to assist with timely and effective communication. The resources included may be adapted to fit PSU needs, helping leaders keep all stakeholders informed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ): 2026 ELA Standard Course of Study

How have the standards changed?
In addition to the standard-objective format, major changes include new strands, foundational skills K-12, and integration. View the Crosswalk to learn more.

Is it aligned to the science of Reading?
Yes! The 2026 SCOS is deeply aligned and informed by this body of research. Foundational skills are outlined in a broad scope and sequence and extend to Grade 12.

What professional learning is available?
PD has already begun and will continue throughout implementation. To view past or upcoming PD, visit our repository!

What resources will be provided?
Several resources are now available! Future resources include Unpacking, Glossary, Progressions, and more! See the installation timelines for details.

When are we required to implement it?
The current 2017 ELA SCOS remains in place for the 25-26 and 26-27 SYs. PSUs are not required to implement until the 27-28 SY.

What about state assessments?
Contact Testing Development with specific questions regarding EOG/EOCs. State assessments for the 2027-2028 school year will be aligned to the 2026 ELA SCOS.

How can I stay updated?
Join the ELA listserv to receive First Friday Standards Updates straight to your inbox! On social media? Follow us!

Who do I contact with questions?
Contact the NCDPI ELA Team with specific questions regarding the 2026 ELA Standard Course of Study. We are happy to support you in your implementation of the 2026 ELA Standards!
ELA Standards Installation Timeline: 2025-2026 School Year
On January 8, 2026, the North Carolina State Board of Education (SBE) approved the 2026 ELA Standard Course of Study (SCOS). A timeline of activities for installation is presented below. Installation is slated to be completed in July 2027. Public school units (PSUs) are not required to implement the 2026 ELA SCOS until the 2027-2028 school year. The current 2017 ELA SCOS remains in place for classroom instruction for the 2025-2026 and 2026-2027 school years. State assessments for the 2027-2028 school year will be aligned to the 2026 ELA SCOS.
25-26 Timeline

* Installation Timeline, Crosswalk, FAQ, and other resources released.

* Unpacking Documents, Glossary, and other resources released

* Registration for in-person regional PD opens for PSU ELA Leaders

* In-person PD takes place in each region and is also provided virtually
