# Settings Views Context

## Purpose
Store settings, administrative configuration, compliance, accessibility, and operational monitoring screens.

## Contents
- Accessibility and privacy settings.
- Admin setup, IT integration, AI usage, topic boundaries, data retention, and PII review screens.

## Conventions
- Use settings screens for administrative review and operational controls.
- Keep destructive or approval actions explicit and role-gated.
- Prefer list/form layouts that work on iPad and macOS.
- Log settings screen additions or edits in `EduAssistall/Views/Settings/LOG.md`.
